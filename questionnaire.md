# HackOnVibe — Project Questionnaire

**1. What does your application/service do?**

LauchKix is an AI-powered platform that generates complete launch marketing kits for mobile apps in under 60 seconds. It transforms basic app details (name, description, target audience, tone) into 8 professional marketing sections: App Store & Google Play listings with character-optimized titles/descriptions, ASO keyword packs, a 7-day social media calendar (X, LinkedIn, Instagram), launch email campaigns, community launch posts (Reddit, Product Hunt, Indie Hackers), landing page copy, press & media blurbs, and multi-format export (Markdown, JSON, or copyable text). It uses Gemini 2.0 Flash via OpenRouter with structured JSON output, tone customization, and goal-focused generation; users can regenerate individual sections without losing other content.

**2. Who is the target audience?**

Primary: indie mobile app developers (solo founders) lacking marketing expertise or budget; small app studios (2-10 people) without marketing teams; student and hackathon teams launching their first commercial apps; and SMB internal teams promoting B2B or internal mobile tools. Secondary: freelance app marketers, marketing agencies serving multiple clients, and accelerator programs providing launch tools to their cohorts.

**3. Which countries are the expected buyers of this service?**

Tier 1 (primary, ~70% of revenue): United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia. Tier 2 (growth, ~20%): Germany, France, Netherlands, Singapore, India. Tier 3 (emerging, ~10%): Spain, Brazil, UAE, South Korea, Japan (with future localization).

**4. Who are your competitors?**

Direct: Copy.ai and Jasper.ai (general-purpose AI copywriting, not mobile-specific), and direct use of ChatGPT/Claude (no structured output). Indirect: Fiverr/Upwork freelancers ($150-500/launch), ASO tools like App Radar and Sensor Tower (keyword/analytics only), and marketing agencies ($2,000-10,000/launch). Key gap: no competitor combines AI generation + mobile-specific optimization + structured kit format + sub-60-second speed + section regeneration + sub-$20/mo pricing.

**5. What is your advantage?**

Mobile-first specialization (built-in App Store/Play Store character limits and ASO keyword strategy); speed-to-value (60 seconds, 30-60x faster than alternatives); a structured kit format (8 interconnected sections, ready to use); smart section regeneration (no full-regeneration friction); a developer-friendly experience (multi-format export, guided forms, real-time character warnings); and a strong price-to-value ratio (70-80% cheaper than Copy.ai/Jasper, 10-20x cheaper than freelancers, plus a free tier).
